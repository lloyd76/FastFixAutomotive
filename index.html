Ian's Story

Ian's journey in automotive repair began at just 16 years old, studying at Bolton College before gaining hands-on experience at Land Rover Bury and Cowley Hill Services.

Over a year ago, Ian founded Fast Fix Automotive with a vision to provide reliable, mobile mechanic services. What started as a solo operation handling both mechanics and roadside assistance has grown steadily, with plans to expand the team in the near future.

Our mission is simple: "We cater for all cars and vans—all makes, models and repairs." No job is too big or too small for our team.
